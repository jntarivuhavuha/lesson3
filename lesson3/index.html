Site Purpose

The purpose of Port Vila chamber of commerce site is to encourage trade, investment, and providing economic opportunities for all business owners, entrepreneurs, and communities. Makes a unique and significant contribution to the livelyhood of the people of Port Vila by representing all in dialog with the goverment and other business partners for the betterment of the communities.
