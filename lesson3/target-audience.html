Target Audience

The Port Vila chamber of commerce site target audience includes goverment institutions, private sectors, all business owners and future potential business owners. The target audience includes help small businesses advertise more efficiently, find customers for their business and help their business succeed.

Personas

Joel Tarivuhavuha

Joel owns a toyota hiace bus that provides transportation to the people of Port Vila. He employed a driver that drives the toyota hiace bus to service the people. Joel didn't attend college or university but attend the pathway world wide program. what he has learn from the pathway program he decided to take a loan from one of the local bank and bought a toyota hiace bus and start a transportation business.

Today Joel is 39 years old and soon he will turn 40 years old. He has a wife with 2 kids. A daughter who is 11 years old and a son who is 4 years old.

As a owner of a transportation business in port vila, Joel is very concerned about the poor road condition, increasing prices of fuels, and increase prices of vehicle parts which sometimes makes it a bit difficult for his driver to archieve their monthly target.

William Boehula

William Boehula is a resident of port vila city. He is the owner of Bountiful Vanuatu Tours and Transfer. He didn't attend university but went on a mission for the church of Jesus Christ of Latter day saints for 2 years serving the people of Philiphines.

When he return home from his mission, the experience he gain from his mission has help him to start a tour and transfer business in port vila. Today he has become one of the best tours and transfer operator in Port Vila. He owns 5 toyota hiace Bus, employed around 10 people and has expand his business across the country.

As his business expand across the country one of his challenges or major concerned is about the cross border transaction of traveller who came from other countries. A significant number of countries now have transfer pricing legislation in place. Accordingly, businesses which trade internationally need coordinated advice and protection if they are to achieve their commercial aims.
